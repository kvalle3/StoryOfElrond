Elven Power

This is a representation of the power to be able to communicate with animals nonverbally. Every circle that pops up is the representation of the message transmitted back and forth to the animal and the elf. The squares are one party, and the circles are the other party of this conversation. The 'Hello' is just an example of what one message myight say.
